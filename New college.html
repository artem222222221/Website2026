Applying for an IT Course

After finishing school, I decided to study Information Technology because I enjoy working with computers and learning about technology. I searched for different colleges and courses before choosing Cardiff and Vale College.

I completed the online application form and provided information about my education and interests in IT. After applying, I attended an interview where I explained why I wanted to join the course.

I was very happy when I received confirmation that I had been accepted onto the IT Level 2 course. It was an important step towards my future career.

My First Day at College

On my first day at college, I felt excited and nervous because everything was new to me. I met new classmates and teachers who were friendly and welcoming.

During the day, with Anthony we learned about the college rules, our timetable, and the subjects we would study. I also explored the classrooms and computer rooms where we would complete our practical work.

My first day gave me a positive impression of college life and motivated me to work hard and improve my IT skills in different ways and subjects.
