My Overall Opinion

By the end of this course at New College, I feel that I have improved both my technical and personal skills. Throughout the year I have learned many different areas of IT, including web development, coding, spreadsheets, databases and multimedia.

One of the things I enjoyed most about the course was learning databases, spreadsheets, and how to create websites using HTML and CSS. I became more confident when designing pages, organising layouts, and improving the appearance of my work.

The course also helped me improve my problem-solving skills and taught me how to work independently as well as part of a team. The assignments and practical tasks gave me useful experience that I can use in future studies and careers.

Overall, I believe the course was enjoyable, educational and helpful for developing my IT knowledge. It has encouraged me to continue learning more about technology and web development in the future.
